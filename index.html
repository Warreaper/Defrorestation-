You may be asking yourself well what is it? why would i care? Well heres why ...

Deforestatio is caused primarily by human activities like farming, logging, and urban development, disrupts habitats, leading to biodiversity loss, soil erosion, altered waterpatterns, and increased carbon emissions, exacerbating climate change. It's crucial for everyone to care about deforestation because forests maintain Earth's ecological balance, providing habitat for species, regulating climate, and offering essential resources like clean air and water,which is vital for human well-being. Protecting forests isn't just about environmental preservation; it's essential for ensuring the long-term sustainability of life on our planet.
